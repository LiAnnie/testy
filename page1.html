Hobbies


  • Writing

    I enjoy writing stories because it helps me express myself. Spoooky stories are fun for me to read and write because there is an exciting shivery feeling.

We formed a group with the people that we met on the game, and although we don't play Wizard101 anymore, we've been playing together ever since. I mainly spend my free
    time on Genshin Impact these days.

  • Watching Videos

    I usually have a video running in the background while I work because it helps me to stay awake. I don't often know what's going on in the videos, but I still enjoy listening to the
    sound of people talking. Sometimes, I watch television series like NCIS, but most of the time
    I just watch Youtube.

  • Drawing

    Ever since I was six, I've been going to art classes. It made me enjoy drawing and other
    forms of art. Sometimes when I'm in online class, I have a sketchbook open in front of me. I enjoy sketching realistic art the most, because the end result is super satisfying to look at.

  • Gambling

    I enjoy playing cards with my family for money. Each round won is one dollar, and each
    round lost loses one dollar. The most anyone has ever won is five dollars, but it's still fun to play with something on the line.
